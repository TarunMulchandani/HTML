The Best Movies According to Tarun

My top 5 movies of all-time.

1. 3 Idiots

A heartwarming and thought-provoking film that challenges the flaws of the education system. It blends humor, emotion, and life lessons with unforgettable performances and quotes.

2. Zindagi na Milege Dubara

A soul-refreshing journey of friendship, self-discovery, and living in the moment. Its beautiful cinematography, music, and subtle life messages make it deeply relatable.

3. Piku

A gentle and charming story about a daughter’s bond with her aging father, full of emotional authenticity. It stands out for its simplicity, strong performances, and heartfelt humor.

4. Karwaan

A soulful road trip film that deals with grief, purpose, and unexpected companionship. Its subtle storytelling and calm vibe offer a refreshing break from typical Bollywood drama.

5. The Intern

A feel-good film about second chances, age, and mentorship in the modern workplace. Robert De Niro and Anne Hathaway’s chemistry beautifully bridges the generation gap with warmth and humor.
